Understanding CS2 Binds and Aliases

Binds and aliases are powerful tools in CS2 that allow you to customize your gameplay experience. With CONFIGEN, you can easily create complex configurations without needing to remember syntax.

Binds assign actions to specific keys. For example, you can bind a key to buy weapons, throw grenades in specific ways, or execute complex movement sequences.

Aliases are custom commands that can execute multiple actions with a single keypress. They're perfect for creating sophisticated utility sequences or movement tricks.
